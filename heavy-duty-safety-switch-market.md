<p><ul><li>Fusible Disconnect</li><li>Non-fusible Disconnect</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Heavy Duty Safety Switch market is segmented into two main types: fusible disconnects and non-fusible disconnects. Fusible disconnects include fuses for overcurrent protection, allowing safe disconnection of electrical circuits while providing a backup against surges. In contrast, non-fusible disconnects lack fuses and rely on circuit breakers or other systems for protection. Both types serve critical roles in ensuring the safe operation of electrical systems by providing reliable switching and isolation capabilities for maintenance and emergency situations.</p></p>
