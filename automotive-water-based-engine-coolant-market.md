<p><p>The automotive water-based engine coolant market features several types, primarily categorized into ethylene glycol coolant and propylene glycol coolant. Ethylene glycol coolant is widely used due to its excellent heat transfer properties and low freezing point. Propylene glycol coolant, although less effective in heat transfer, is non-toxic and suitable for environmentally sensitive applications. Other formulations may include organic acid technology (OAT) coolants or hybrid coolants that combine various chemical properties for specific vehicle requirements, catering to diverse engine designs and regulations.</p></p>
