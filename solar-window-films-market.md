<p><p>The solar window films market offers various types based on their properties and aesthetics. Clear (non-reflective) films provide UV protection and heat reduction without altering the appearance of windows, making them ideal for maintaining visibility. Dyed (non-reflective) films enhance privacy and reduce glare while absorbing solar energy, creating a cooler interior. Vacuum coated (reflective) films utilize metallic coatings to reflect sunlight effectively, providing superior heat rejection and energy efficiency, making them popular for commercial and residential applications seeking energy savings and comfort.</p></p>
