<p><p>The artificial sports turf market comprises three main types: polypropylene turf, polyethylene turf, and nylon turf. Polypropylene turf is lightweight and cost-effective, suitable for temporary installations and lower-impact sports. Polyethylene turf offers a softer, more durable surface, making it ideal for high-impact sports like soccer and football. Nylon turf is known for its exceptional resilience and longevity, often used in high-performance applications, including professional sports fields. Each type serves specific needs based on durability, cost, and intended usage.</p></p>
