<p><p>The wall-mounted gas boiler market is primarily segmented into condensing and non-condensing types. Condensing boilers are designed to capture and utilize exhaust gases, improving energy efficiency and lowering emissions, making them environmentally friendly and cost-effective for consumers. In contrast, non-condensing boilers operate at higher temperatures, releasing more waste heat, leading to lower efficiency and higher operational costs. The choice between these types depends on factors like installation space, energy efficiency requirements, and regulatory standards in different regions.</p></p>
