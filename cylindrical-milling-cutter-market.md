<p><p>Cylindrical milling cutters are essential tools in machining processes, classified primarily into two types: straight teeth and spiral teeth. Straight teeth cutters feature parallel cutting edges, ideal for producing flat surfaces and efficient cutting in softer materials. In contrast, spiral teeth cutters have an angled tooth design, offering smoother cutting action and reduced vibration, making them suitable for harder materials and complex shapes. Both types play critical roles in enhancing precision and efficiency in various manufacturing applications.</p></p>
