<p><p>The LAN cable market primarily consists of various categories, including Cat 5, Cat 5e, Cat 6, and Cat 7 cables. Cat 5 cables support speeds up to 100 Mbps, making them suitable for basic networking needs. Cat 5e enhances performance, reducing cross-talk and supporting speeds up to 1 Gbps. Cat 6 cables offer increased bandwidth, suitable for 10 Gbps over short distances. Cat 7 cables provide higher shielding and support advanced networking applications with improved speeds and less interference, making them ideal for data centers.</p></p>
