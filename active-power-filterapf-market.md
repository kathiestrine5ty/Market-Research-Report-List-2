<p><strong>The Active Power Filter(APF) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Active Power Filter</li><li>Series Active Power Filter</li><li>Hybrid Active Power Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active Power Filters (APFs) are devices that enhance power quality by compensating for harmonic distortion, voltage fluctuations, and reactive power in electrical systems. The market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Shunt Active Power Filter**: Connected parallel to the load, it absorbs or supplies reactive power and eliminates harmonics. </p><p>2. **Series Active Power Filter**: Placed in series with the load, it mitigates voltage disturbances and manages harmonics. </p><p>3. **Hybrid Active Power Filters**: Combine both shunt and series functions for enhanced performance, improving overall power quality and efficiency.</p></p>
