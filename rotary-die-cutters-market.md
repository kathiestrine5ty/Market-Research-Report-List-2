<p><ul><li>Semi Rotary Die Cutter</li><li>Full Rotary Die Cutter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary die cutters market consists of two primary types: semi-rotary and full rotary die cutters. Semi-rotary die cutters utilize a combination of rotary and flat die cutting, allowing for versatility in production while accommodating various materials and thicknesses. Full rotary die cutters, on the other hand, maintain a continuous rotary motion, offering high-speed cutting for large volumes and precise designs. Both types are essential in packaging, labels, and other sectors requiring efficient and accurate cutting solutions.</p></p>
