<p><p>The modular cutting machine market is categorized into four main types: water jet, laser, oxyacetylene, and others. Water jet cutting uses high-pressure water mixed with abrasives for precision slicing without thermal distortion. Laser cutting employs focused light beams for intricate designs and clean cuts, suitable for various materials. Oxyacetylene cutting utilizes a flame from burning oxygen and acetylene to melt and cut metals, especially thick gauges. Other methods may include plasma cutting and mechanical cutting techniques, diversifying application across industries.</p></p>
