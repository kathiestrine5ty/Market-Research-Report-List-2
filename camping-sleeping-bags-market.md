<p><ul><li>Natural Down Fill</li><li>Synthetic Fill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The camping sleeping bags market is primarily divided into two types: natural down fill and synthetic fill. Natural down fill insulation, sourced from goose or duck feathers, offers excellent warmth-to-weight ratios, compressibility, and durability, making it ideal for cooler conditions. However, it loses insulating properties when wet. Conversely, synthetic fill insulation, made from polyester fibers, provides better moisture resistance, dries quickly, and is often more affordable, though typically bulkier. Each type caters to different preferences and camping conditions.</p></p>
