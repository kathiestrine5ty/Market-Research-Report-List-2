<p><p>The commercial beer dispensers market includes three primary types: Direct Draw System, Air Cooled System, and Glycol Cooled System. </p><p>The Direct Draw System dispenses beer directly from the kegs, typically used in establishments with minimal distances. The Air Cooled System uses ambient air to cool the beer lines, ideal for small venues. The Glycol Cooled System employs a glycol mixture for efficient cooling over longer distances, making it suitable for larger setups and ensuring consistent beer temperature and quality.</p></p>
