<p><p>The Gigabit Ethernet camera market includes two primary types: line scan cameras and area scan cameras. Line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, making them ideal for applications requiring continuous data capture, such as quality inspection in manufacturing. Area scan cameras, on the other hand, capture an entire frame in a single shot, making them suitable for a wide range of applications, including surveillance and machine vision. Both types leverage Gigabit Ethernet for high-speed data transmission.</p></p>
