<p><ul><li>ArF Dry Light Sources</li><li>ArF Immersion Light Sources</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ArF excimer lasers market is divided into two main types: ArF dry light sources and ArF immersion light sources. ArF dry light sources operate in air and are primarily used in photolithography processes for semiconductor manufacturing, offering high resolution and efficiency. In contrast, ArF immersion light sources utilize a liquid medium to enhance imaging resolution beyond the diffraction limit, critical for producing smaller semiconductor features. Both types are essential for advancing lithography technology in the electronics industry.</p></p>
