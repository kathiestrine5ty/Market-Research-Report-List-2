<p><p>Thin film metrology systems encompass several market types based on the characteristics of the films being measured. Opaque films are non-transparent, allowing for precise thickness and uniformity assessments using techniques like ellipsometry. Transparent films, on the other hand, require methods that can penetrate the material to extract data on thickness and refractive index. Thick films, often used in electronics, present unique challenges in metrology due to their greater dimensions. The "Others" category includes specialty films that may require tailored measurement approaches.</p></p>
