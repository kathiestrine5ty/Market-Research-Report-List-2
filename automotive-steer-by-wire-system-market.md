<p><p>The automotive steer-by-wire system market features two main types: pure electronic and backup mechanical systems. Pure electronic systems rely solely on electronic controls, enhancing responsiveness and allowing for advanced features like variable steering ratios. Backup mechanical systems, on the other hand, integrate a mechanical link as a fail-safe, ensuring safety in case of electronic failure. This hybrid approach balances innovation with reliability, appealing to manufacturers focused on enhancing vehicle performance while maintaining stringent safety standards.</p></p>
