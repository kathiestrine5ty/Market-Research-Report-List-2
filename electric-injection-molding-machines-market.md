<p><p>Electric injection molding machines are categorized into vertical, horizontal, and other designs based on their configuration and operational characteristics. Vertical machines feature a design where the injection unit is positioned above the clamp, facilitating space efficiency and easy access for loading materials. Horizontal machines, the most common type, have a horizontal injection unit and are ideal for higher production volumes. Other types include hybrid models that combine hydraulic and electric features, offering versatility and energy efficiency tailored to specific manufacturing needs.</p></p>
