<p><p>The Train Wheel Magnetic Sensor market is divided into two main types: passive and active railway wheel sensors. Passive railway wheel sensors rely on external magnetic fields and do not require an independent power source, making them cost-effective and easy to integrate. In contrast, active railway wheel sensors generate their own magnetic field, providing enhanced sensitivity and accuracy in detecting wheel movements. Both types play crucial roles in monitoring train performance, improving safety, and enabling real-time data collection for railway systems.</p></p>
