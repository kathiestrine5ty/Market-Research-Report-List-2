<p><p>The antidegradants market comprises primarily two types: antioxidants and antiozonants. Antioxidants are chemical compounds that prevent oxidation, thereby protecting materials, especially rubbers and plastics, from degradation due to environmental factors and heat. Antiozonants, on the other hand, are used to shield materials from ozone-induced damage, a prevalent concern in outdoor applications. Together, these additives enhance the longevity and reliability of products in industries like automotive, aerospace, and consumer goods, ensuring better performance and durability in challenging conditions.</p></p>
