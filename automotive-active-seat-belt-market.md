<p><p>The automotive active seat belt market encompasses various types designed to enhance occupant safety. Two-point belts provide basic restraint, while three-point belts are the standard, combining a lap and shoulder strap for better security. Four-point belts offer improved stability with additional anchorage points, ideal for racing or specialized vehicles. Five-point belts, commonly used in motorsports, secure the occupant more effectively. Six-point and seven-point belts provide even greater safety by distributing forces across more points, reducing injury risk during collisions.</p></p>
