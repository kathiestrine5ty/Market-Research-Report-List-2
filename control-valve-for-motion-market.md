<p><ul><li>Rotary</li><li>Linear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Control valves in the motion market are essential for regulating flow and movement in various applications. Rotary control valves use a quarter-turn mechanism to manage the flow direction, pressure, and rate, commonly found in HVAC systems and process control. In contrast, linear control valves operate through a sliding motion to adjust the flow rate, often utilized in applications requiring precise volume control, such as water treatment and chemical processing. Both types enhance system efficiency and performance across industries.</p></p>
